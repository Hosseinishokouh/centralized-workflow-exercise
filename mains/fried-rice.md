# Fried rice

A popular Asian dish with a mix of fresh ingredients and spice.

## Ingredients (4 portions)

-**4 dl** of cooked rice
-**400 g** of chicken/beef/tofu
-**3** egg
-**1** onion
-**150 g** peas
-**4** garlic
-**1** piece of ginger
-**1 tbs** sambal oelek
-**2 tbs** oyster sauce

# Instructions

Mix everthing together and fry it!
